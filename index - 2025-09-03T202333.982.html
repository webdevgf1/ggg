WHAT IS DittoLLM

DittoLLM is a powerful text-to-3D AI model that transforms your text descriptions or images into high-quality 3D models. Just like its Pokemon namesake, DittoLLM possesses the remarkable ability to transform into anything - and similarly, it's constantly learning and adapting new capabilities. Through continuous training, DittoLLM evolves its skillset, learning to create increasingly sophisticated 3D objects, characters, props, and environments for games, animation, and virtual worlds.

PROMPTING TIPS & TRICKS

Be Specific

Instead of "car", try "sleek red sports car with chrome wheels and tinted windows"

Add Context

Include materials, style, and setting. Example: "wooden desk in minimalist Scandinavian style"

Use References

Mention specific styles or artists. Example: "art deco style lamp with geometric patterns"

Define Scale

Include size references. Example: "miniature fairy house the size of a coffee cup"
